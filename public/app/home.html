arrange with orrange

for too long, musicians have relied on printed chord charts to remind them about a song's structure, arrangement, chord progressions. annotating these documents meant messy scribbles and overload of information. nobody wants to keep track of every song you've ever had to learn, espcially in today's digital age. that's where orrange comes in.

how to use this site

begin by clicking on the "Add Song" tab above. Enter as much or as little information as you like. When you're done, hit save. Your song will show up in the "Song Library" tab.
